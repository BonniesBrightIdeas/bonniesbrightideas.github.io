HELLO!

I’m Bonnie Ward, a UX-UI Designer by certification from the University of California, Irvine with a Bachelors in Business Administration. During the past 10 years, I’ve worked in diverse industries and have exposed myself to various challenges allowing me to develop my ability to connect with people on a deeper level. The experience has allowed me to empathize and get to the heart of what drives people so that I can craft more inclusive and meaningful experiences. I love working on problems from the ideation stage and seeing them through completion with pixel-perfect designs.

As a designer, I want to make great products that have a clearly defined sense of purpose, deliver value in a singularly focused way, and do so as well or better than any other product in the marketplace. To keep my ideas fresh, I stay up to date with trends, design methods, and industry tools.
