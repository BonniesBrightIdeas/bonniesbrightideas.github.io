CASE STUDY | AUGUST 2020

Redesigning a Nonprofit Website

User Research of the www.elihome.org website to discover pain points and needs of both the user and the organization.

Problem

People are less likely to donate to a website that doesn’t look professional and has unclear impacts made by donations.

Solution

By improving the website flow, focusing content, and creating a cohesive design, it will make the nonprofit appear professional; therefore, improve donation conversion, as well as, build a larger support base.
